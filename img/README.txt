Drop your photos here, then uncomment the matching <img> tag in the HTML.

  hero.jpg    → cover hero (inside .cover-hero in index.html)
  day01.jpg   → Day 1 home card   (inside .day-card-image in index.html)
  day02.jpg   → Day 2 home card
  ...
  day10.jpg   → Day 10 home card

Recommended sizes:
  hero.jpg   ~1200 x 1500 (tall, portrait-ish — it fills a 500px-tall banner)
  dayNN.jpg  ~800 x 500  (landscape — fills a full-width 200px-tall card image)
