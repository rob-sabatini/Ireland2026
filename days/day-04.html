Itinerary
9:30am Breakfast · leave Galway 10:00am
10:45am Cliffs of Moher — morning visit

Walk south toward Hag's Head immediately — this is where crowds thin out. O'Brien's Tower for elevated perspective.

1:00pm Doolin lunch

Doolin Café or Gus O'Connor's. 10 min from the Cliffs.

2:30pm Doolin Cave

Longest free-hanging stalactite in the northern hemisphere. ~45 mins.

3:30pm The Burren self-guided drive

Poulnabrone Dolmen, Caherconnell Stone Fort, Corcomroe Abbey if energy allows.

6:30pm Back to The Dean — freshen up
8:15pm Ard Bia at Nimmo's dinner

Request downstairs river-facing table on arrival. Ref: B32TLMCM.

Notes

→ Leave Galway by 10am. Drive southwest on N67/R478 toward Liscannor — ~45 mins to the Cliffs.

→ August wind on the cliffs can be significant — secure hats and light layers for everyone.

→ Go south toward Hag's Head on arrival — this is where it becomes genuinely special.

→ Deb note — Ard Bia has lamb, beef and chicken options. Ask the server to guide you.
