Itinerary
7:00am Early breakfast · leave Galway 7:45am

Free parking. You will be weighed at check-in — weight distribution on the 9-seat plane. Normal.

8 minutes. Extraordinary aerial views. Booking 520A4N.

One of the great pub settings in Ireland. Overlooking the harbour.

Traditional Aran knitwear. Good gifts. Bring card and cash.

4:15pm Flight 0166 — return to Inverin

Booking 520A4N.

Notes

→ Leave Galway by 7:45am sharp. Drive west on N59/R336 to Inverin — about 60 minutes.

→ The plane is a Britten-Norman Islander — 9 seats, twin engine, 8 minute flight.

→ Bring cash euros — carriage tour, Joe Watty's, Aran Sweater Market. Limited ATM on island.

→ No fence at Dún Aonghasa cliff edge. Stay well back, especially with the kids. 100m straight down.

→ Be at Inis Mór airport by 4:00pm for the 4:15pm return. Don't cut it close.

→ Weather contingency: if flights cancelled due to low cloud — Wildlands Moycullen (15 min from Galway) is backup.
