Itinerary
9:30am Breakfast · leave Galway 10:00am on N59

Scenic drive through Maam Cross and Inagh Valley. Stop at viewpoints.

11:45am Clifden Bike Shop — 3 ebikes

Sky Road loop, 13km. Clifden Castle ruins detour. Helmets and route map included. Ref: KJGL-160726.

1:30pm Return bikes — drive to Mitchell's
2:00pm Mitchell's Restaurant lunch — Clifden

Walk-in or call ahead.

3:45pm Kylemore Abbey

Grounds, abbey interior, Victorian walled garden. Closes 6pm — arrive by 3:45pm.

5:15pm Drive back to Galway — ~1.5 hours
8:30pm Kai Restaurant dinner

Seasonal Irish menu. Request cosy table with atmosphere. Ref: BTK7CFYC.
